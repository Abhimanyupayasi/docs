# Process Scheduler in NextGen QE

The **Scheduler** feature in **NextGen QE** allows you to plan and automate test process executions at predefined dates and times.  
This helps teams execute testing workflows automatically without manual intervention.

---

## Steps to Schedule a Process

### Step 1: Open the Execute Tab
Navigate to the **Execute** tab from the main navigation menu.

---

### Step 2: Go to the Scheduler Tab
Select the **Scheduler** tab to manage or create new process schedules.

---

### Step 3: Create a New Scheduler
Click on **Create New Scheduler** and fill in the required details:

- **Process *** → Select the process you want to schedule.  
- **Date *** → Choose the execution date (MM/DD/YYYY format).  
- **Time *** → Set the desired execution time.

<img src="/web-testing/imgs/scheduler.png" alt="Scheduler setup for process execution in NextGen QE" />

---

### Step 4: Save and Schedule
Once all details are filled, save the scheduler configuration.  
Your selected process will now execute automatically at the specified date and time.

---

✅ **Result:**  
Your process has been successfully scheduled for automated execution.
