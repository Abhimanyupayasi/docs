# Reports in NextGen QE

The **Reports** section in **NextGen QE** provides detailed insights into all executed test entities — including **feature-level test suites**, **product/module/release test suites**, **processes**, and **use cases**.  
Once these executions are completed, the results and analytics can be accessed here for review and analysis.

---

## Accessing Reports

### Step 1: Open NextGen QE
Log in to **NextGen QE** from the main dashboard.

---

### Step 2: Navigate to the Reports Tab
From the main navigation menu, go to the **Reports** tab to view all execution summaries, performance statistics, and result details.

✅ **Result:**  
You can now analyze detailed execution results, verify outcomes, and export reports for auditing or team review.
